SUBMISSIONS

We are interested in representing works of creative non-fiction and fiction, specifically mysteries, suspense, thillers and the romance genre.

You needn't bother with a self-addressed stamped envelope for your queries. We pride ourselves on being a 'green' company and would prefer you query us via email only.

Email the first ten pages of your manuscript with a 1-page query letter to the address below. Please no attachments.


Email: submissions@jplitagency.com


Please remember that a good query letter should mimic the hardcover flap copy or paperback cover copy you would expect to see on your book should it be published.

It goes without saying: Review your material. Do your research and do a halfway decent job on your query letter and the strength of your writing and your personality will shine through.

Good writing is good writing, the label they'll put on it in the library regardless.

So, no pedestrian prose, no predictable plots. Remember, if it catches our eye, there's a good chance it'll catch an editor's.
