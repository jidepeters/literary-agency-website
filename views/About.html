ABOUT US

We're first-class agents on the hunt for first-class writers.

Have you written a manuscript that captures imaginations, engages minds, elicits response? Sign up with us. Let us make your publishing dreams come true.

You with the gift to craft words into fresh and engaging content. With JP Literary Agency, you're in good hands, beause we know what works, what doesn't and why.


What are we hoping to find?

A writer with a compelling voice.

An author's voice is often imitated, but a truly original voice can never be duplicated.

Undoubtedly, voice is one of the most difficult writing terms to define and pinpoint, but we at JP Literary Agency like to think of it as a sensibility with which an author writes. It's a perspective, an outlook on the world, a personality and style that is recognizable even out of context. You could drop randomly into a David Sedaris story or an Ernest Hemingway novel and probably guess the author within a few paragraphs.

Why?

Easy. They have strong, unique voices.

It's tough to get in the door. Show us up-front proof of the quality of your writing, the strength of your concept.

Be that author who brings the reader into an imaginary yet 'real' world, creates compelling characters who live there, make us root for them, while taking us on a rip-roaring yarn.

Always, the literary market is looking for something fresh and original, a new voice. Maybe that new voice is you.
